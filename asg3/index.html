How to Play

  • W, A, S, D - Move around
  • Click & Drag - Rotate camera
  • E, Q, Z, X - Tilt camera
  • Space & Left Ctrl - Float or descend
  • P to place down and remove stone blocks
  • Your objective: Find the creeper hiding in the world.
  • Once you find him, click on him to win!
